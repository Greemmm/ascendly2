About Us

Ascendly is a student gaming stream dedicated to helping players improve, learn smart strategies, and enjoy gameplay. We focus on providing simple, practical gaming tips while creating an entertaining and engaging streaming experience. Our goal is to build a community where players can learn, grow, and have fun together.

Stream Design

Stream Flow

Our stream usually starts around 7:00 AM and will be ending at exactly 10:05 AM. We follow a structured flow to keep our stream smooth, organized, and fun for everyone. The stream will be happening on the platform Youtube.

Time Duration Segment Goals
7:00 10 Minutes Setup Starting Soon screen appears while we check audio, camera, overlays, and game capture.
7:10 10 Minutes Intro We go live, greet viewers, and give quick updates or shoutouts.
7:20 10 Minutes Gaming Tips Short segment where we share useful strategies and tips for the game.
7:30 1 Hour Game Focus Main gameplay begins. We focus on completing objectives while interacting with chat during slow moments.
8:30 10 Minutes Community Break Quick break to rest, hydrate, and chat with the audience.
8:40 1 Hour Game Focus We continue gameplay with new goals or viewer participation if planned.
9:40 25 Minutes Promotion/Additional Activities Wrap up the game, talk with the community, announce updates, and remind viewers to follow our socials.
10:05 5 Minutes Outro Thank viewers, recap the stream, and end smoothly.

Promotional Plan



We will use social media to promote our streams by consistently posting gaming tips, highlight clips, countdowns, schedules, and live updates across Facebook, TikTok, and Youtube to grow engagement, increase viewers, and build a strong gaming community.
