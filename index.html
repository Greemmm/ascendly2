About Us

Ascendly is a student gaming stream dedicated to helping players improve, learn smart strategies, and enjoy gameplay. We focus on providing simple, practical gaming tips while creating an entertaining and engaging streaming experience. Our goal is to build a community where players can learn, grow, and have fun together.

Stream Design

Stream Flow

Our stream usually starts around 7:00 AM and will be ending at exactly 10:05 AM. We follow a structured flow to keep our stream smooth, organized, and fun for everyone. The stream will be happening on the platform Youtube.

Time Duration Segment Goals
7:00 10 Minutes Setup The stream is live, but the camera/mic is off. Use a branded Starting Soon screen with a countdown timer. Check all software (audio levels, game capture, overlays). Review chat rules. Play royalty-free music.
7:10 10 Minutes Intro Go live! High-energy greeting and shout out recent followers and subscribers.
7:20 10 Minutes Gaming Tips Gaming tips are important in a stream because they are a form of engaging content that helps attract and retain viewers, build a community, and establish the streamer's credibility and expertise. Viewers often tune in specifically to learn and improve their own gameplay.
7:30 1 Hour Game Focus The main action begins. Focus intensely on the game objective. Engage with chat primarily during downtime (loading screens, dying, inventory management). Answer a Question of the Day (QOTD) if you have one.
8:30 10 Minutes Community Break Streaming is a demanding activity, and taking breaks is vital to prevent burnout and maintain a positive attitude.
8:40 1 Hour Game Focus Return to the game, perhaps with a new strategy or objective. If playing with viewers, start the "Viewer Games" segment here. Keep the energy high and recap what happened before the break.
9:40 25 Minutes Promotion/Additional Activities To finish strong, we will complete the current objective or find a good stopping point, then transition to a Community Chat session to review performance and plan the next stream; before we wrap up, please remember to follow all my social media, and a final thanks goes to the mods and community as we set up the Raid/Host to send traffic to another streamer.
10:05 5 Minutes Outro It provides a professional closing, helps reinforce your brand, encourages viewers to take the next action (like subscribing or watching another video), and leaves a lasting positive impression. Without a proper outro, a stream can feel abrupt, leaving viewers unsure of what to do next.

Stream Details



7:00 PM — Setup

We prepare the stream by checking audio, camera, overlays, and game capture. A “Starting Soon” screen with music plays while everything loads.


7:10 PM — Intro

We officially go live, greet viewers, and warm up the stream with quick announcements and shoutouts.


7:20 PM — Gaming Tips

We share helpful gaming strategies, mechanics breakdowns, and improvement guides to the game we’re playing.


7:30 PM — Game Focus

The main session begins. We concentrate on completing objectives, leveling up, or progressing through the game while interacting with chat.


8:30 PM — Community Break

A short rest session where we hydrate, stretch, and chat with the community to keep the vibe fresh and avoid burnout.


8:40 PM — Game Focus (Second Session)

We return to gameplay with new strategies or objectives.


9:40 PM — Promotions + Extra Activities

We wrap up ongoing missions, chat about upcoming events, review the game, and remind viewers to follow or join the community.


10:05 PM — Outro

A clean, professional ending that thanks viewers, recaps the stream, and invites them to return next time.
